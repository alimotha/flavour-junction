Traditional Dishes

Biriyani

Biryani is a mixture of delicious yellow rice and either chicken or beef. The rice gets its color from an assortment of spices and herbs, which also adds an incredible flavor. To top it off, it contains perfectly cooked lemons, tomatoes, and potatoes.

Heleem

Haleem is a mixture of a variety of traditional staples used in Pakistani cuisine, such as wheat, barley, minced beef, mutton or chicken. It is a dish that takes a long time to prepare because the lentils and special spices need ample time to properly combine with the minced beef and create a unique paste that will delight the senses.

Aalo Keema

This dish is served in almost every household in Pakistan because it is an excellent blend of potatoes and minced mutton or chicken curry. It’s simple but delicious.

Nihaari

Nihari is one of the most famous stews in the entire country. It is served to guests on important occasions and contains meat that is slow cooked and simmered in spices overnight. The cooking process allows the dish to absorb the flavorful bone marrow fully, creating one of the best-tasting meat dishes in the world.

Ethnic Food

Popsicle

A popsicle is a frozen treat that is typically made by freezing sweetened liquid, such as fruit juice or flavored syrup, in a plastic or wooden mold. Once frozen, the popsicle is removed from the mold and enjoyed on a stick, making it a fun and convenient dessert option.

Cherries

Cherries are a small, round fruit that grow on trees and are known for their sweet and tangy flavor. They have a bright red to dark purple exterior, and a small, hard pit in the center. Cherries are typically in season during the summer months, and are widely available in grocery stores and farmers markets.

Sandwich

A sandwich is a popular type of meal that consists of one or more fillings placed between two slices of bread, which are typically eaten together. The filling can be a variety of meats, cheeses, vegetables, spreads, or any other type of food that can be sandwiched between bread slices.

French Cuisine

French cuisine is known for its sophisticated techniques, rich flavors, and use of high-quality ingredients such as butter, cream, wine, and cheese. Some classic French dishes include Coq au Vin (chicken in wine sauce), Ratatouille (a vegetable stew), Quiche Lorraine (a savory egg and cheese tart), and Croissants (a buttery pastry).
